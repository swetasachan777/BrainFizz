GUIDE TO PLAY :

  • Choose Your Adventure :Select from a variety of categories, ranging from history and science to pop culture and sports. Each category offers a unique set of questions to challenge your knowledge!

  • 2. Set Your Difficulty: Are you a trivia novice or a seasoned expert? Choose your difficulty level before starting the quiz. Easy, Medium, and Hard levels await!

  • Test Your Knowledge: Once you've selected your category and difficulty, it's time to dive into the quiz! You'll be presented with a series of multiple-choice questions. Read each question carefully, select your answer, and hit submit.

  • Score Points: For every correct answer, you'll earn points based on the question's difficulty level.

  • Track Your Progress: Keep an eye on your score as you progress through the quiz.This progress will show your knowledge about that given topic

  • Learn and Have Fun: Whether you're a trivia buff or just looking to test your knowledge, QuizMaster offers an entertaining and educational experience for all players. So, gather your friends, challenge your family, and embark on a trivia adventure like never before!
